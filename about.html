Who We Are

BrainAware is a team of neuroscientists, AI researchers, and medical professionals dedicated to making brain tumor detection more accessible and accurate. Our platform combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with medical expertise to provide reliable brain health analysis.

Our Technology

Using deep learning algorithms trained on thousands of brain scans, our system can detect tumors with 92.4% accuracy. The technology analyzes MRI, CT, and PET scans to identify abnormalities and provide detailed reports within minutes.

Medical Advisory Board

Our solutions are developed in collaboration with leading neurologists and radiologists from top medical institutions, ensuring our technology meets the highest clinical standards.
